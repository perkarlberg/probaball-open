## Coverage reality

Three-named-pundits-per-country is **not achievable** from public sources for
most of the 48 nations: outside a handful of football-media-rich markets,
"who wins 2026" coverage is bookmaker-odds aggregators and AI/economist models,
not named-pundit champion picks. AFC/Oceania and CONMEBOL yielded **zero**
citable local-pundit winner picks. New expert-source countries added here:
Germany, Netherlands, Czechia, Senegal, Mexico, USA.
